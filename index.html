About Us

Al Massriya Al Emaratiya for Import, Export & Commercial Agencies

Al Massriya Al Emaratiya is a multinational import, export, and commercial agency company. Headquartered in Egypt, the company operates across various global markets, connecting businesses with international suppliers, manufacturers, and distribution channels.

With a focus on agility, trust, and digital transformation, the company provides comprehensive trade solutions—from sourcing and shipping to agency representation and market entry strategies. Our aim is to simplify global trade for clients across the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and Europe.
